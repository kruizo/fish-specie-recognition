FishNet+.

Original Paper: Optimized ResNet-50 Transfer Learning with ESRGAN and U-Net for Fish Species Identification in Underwater Environments

FishNet. is a fish classification model designed to help researchers, marine biologists, and enthusiasts identify fish species using computer vision technology. This project aims to promote marine biodiversity awareness, assist in conservation efforts, and make fish identification more accessible to everyone. With an intuitive interface and real-time results, the Fish Specie Classifier bridges the gap between technology and marine science, offering an innovative solution for fish species recognition. The model is trained to classify up to around 18 underwater fish species shown in an image below.

Our proposed model is compared against other state-of-the-art transfer learning models, including MobileNetV2, DenseNet121, InceptionV3, and ResNet-50. Research findings shows that the proposed model achieves the highest accuracy while maintaining a competitive training time.
